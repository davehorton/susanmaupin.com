ABOUT ME

I love helping companies create meaningful connections with their customers.

I am eager to help you and your team achieve your goals, whether that means:

  • ✓working with you to develop your brand and positioning statement;
  • ✓developing high quality marketing programs and collateral;
  • ✓forging growth-accelerating partnerships;
  • ✓designing events that resonate and reinforce your message;
  • ✓crafting online web and mobile experiences for your customers
  • ✓as well as any other marketing needs that you may have.


In my life, as in my work, I am passionate about building purposeful relationships that allow me to experience the world while trying to leave it a better place.

I've worked as a Peace Corps volunteer in a small Morrocan village; pioneered the use of micro-lending to start hundreds of small businesses in the rural Southeast United States; trekked alone and with friends through many of the most remote and beautiful places on earth...could you use some of my energy and "can-do" spirit to move your mission forward?.

How can I help you achieve your mission? Let's connect and get started!
